Food Baby

Food Baby is a product designed for and by hungry students. Utilizing a map, Food Baby shows users the location of nearby events that include free food. These events are created by other users and include information regarding the location, time, date, food offered, as well as whether it is vegetarian and vegan friendly or not. Users can filter their search results based on all of these tags as well.
